Refund Policy

We stand behind our services with a clear and fair refund policy. Learn about our refund procedures and eligibility requirements.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025 Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident you'll love Sapphire Net services. If for any reason you're not completely satisfied with our internet or cable TV services within the first 30 days, we offer a full money-back guarantee.

Full Refund

100% refund of service charges paid for new customers within 30 days

No Questions Asked

Simple cancellation process without lengthy explanations required

Fast Processing

Refunds processed within 5-7 business days of cancellation

2. Refund Eligibility

Eligible Services

The following services are eligible for refunds under our money-back guarantee:

  • Internet Services: All fiber internet plans and speeds
  • Cable TV Services: All channel packages and premium content
  • Bundle Packages: Combined internet and cable TV services
  • Monthly Service Charges: Recurring monthly fees for services

New Customer Qualification

To qualify as a "new customer" for the 30-day guarantee:

  • You must not have had Sapphire Net service at the same address within the past 12 months
  • Service must be activated under a new account
  • You must be the primary account holder
  • Service must be for residential use (business customers have separate terms)

3. Non-Refundable Items

While we offer generous refunds for services, certain charges are non-refundable:

Installation Fees

Standard and custom installation charges are non-refundable after completion

Equipment Fees

Modem, router, and set-top box rental fees for the period used

Late Fees & Penalties

Any late payment fees or penalty charges incurred

Third-Party Services

Premium channels, streaming services, or third-party add-ons

Service Calls

Technician visit fees for repair or maintenance calls

Damage Fees

Charges for damaged, lost, or unreturned equipment

5. Refund Timeline and Methods

Processing Time

Service Cancellation

Same business day when requested before 5 PM ET

Refund Processing

5-7 business days after equipment return (if applicable)

Credit Card Refunds

1-3 business days after processing (may vary by bank)

Bank Transfer Refunds

3-5 business days after processing

Payment Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for service charges:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunded to the card on file
  • Bank Transfer: Refunded to the original bank account
  • Check Payments: Refund check mailed to billing address
  • Gift Cards/Promotions: Refunded as account credit

6. Equipment Return Requirements

Required Returns

All Sapphire Net equipment must be returned in good working condition:

Internet Equipment

  • Fiber optic modem/gateway
  • WiFi router (if provided)
  • Power adapters and cables
  • Ethernet cables

Cable TV Equipment

  • Set-top boxes/DVRs
  • Remote controls
  • Coaxial cables
  • HDMI cables (if provided)

Return Options

Prepaid Return Kit

We'll send a prepaid shipping kit with instructions and packaging materials

Equipment Pickup

Schedule a technician visit for equipment pickup (pickup fee may apply)

Drop-off Location

Return to authorized Sapphire Net service centers or retail partners

7. Partial Refunds and Pro-rating

Service Interruptions

If you experience extended service outages due to our network issues, you may be eligible for service credits:

  • Planned Maintenance: No credit for scheduled maintenance under 4 hours
  • Network Outages: Credit for outages lasting more than 4 continuous hours
  • Equipment Failure: Credit from time of reported issue to resolution
  • Weather/Natural Disasters: Credits evaluated case-by-case

Mid-Cycle Cancellations

For customers who cancel after the 30-day guarantee period:

  • Service charges are pro-rated to the cancellation date
  • No refund for the current billing cycle
  • Equipment rental fees charged through return date
  • Final bill includes all outstanding charges

8. Special Circumstances

Moving/Relocation

If you need to cancel due to moving to an area we don't service:

  • We'll work with you to transfer service to your new location if available
  • Early termination fees may be waived for qualified moves
  • Military deployment orders qualify for special consideration
  • Documentation of move may be required

Service Unavailability

If we cannot provide the level of service originally promised:

  • Option to downgrade to available service tier
  • Pro-rated refund for service level differences
  • Full cancellation and refund if no acceptable alternatives

Billing Errors

If you've been incorrectly charged:

  • Full refund of erroneous charges
  • Credit for any late fees caused by billing errors
  • Correction of future billing statements
  • No time limit on billing error corrections

9. Business Account Refunds

Business customers have different refund terms based on their service agreements:

Small Business (Under 20 Lines)

Similar to residential terms with 30-day guarantee for new customers

Enterprise Accounts

Custom refund terms as specified in individual service agreements

Contract Services

Early termination fees and specific refund clauses apply per contract

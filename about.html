Our Story

Founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to bring the fastest, most reliable internet and entertainment services to communities that deserve better. What started as a small local provider has grown into a trusted regional leader, but we've never forgotten our roots.

We began by recognizing that many areas were underserved by traditional providers, leaving families and businesses with slow, unreliable connections. Our founders invested in building a 100% fiber optic network from the ground up, ensuring every customer receives the premium service they deserve.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 customers across multiple states, but our commitment remains the same: delivering exceptional service with a personal touch that only a community-focused company can provide.

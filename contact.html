Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can service be installed?

Most installations can be completed within 3-5 business days of your order. Emergency installations may be available for an additional fee.

Is there a contract required?

No long-term contracts required! You can cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll love our service.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund your first month's payment.

Do you provide the equipment?

Yes! All necessary equipment including modem, router, and cables are provided free with your service. Professional installation is also included.

Can I keep my current phone number?

Absolutely! We can port your existing phone number to our service at no additional cost. The process typically takes 1-2 business days.

What happens if there's an outage?

Our network is designed for 99.9% uptime with redundant connections. In the rare event of an outage, our team works 24/7 to restore service quickly.

Need Emergency Support?

If you're experiencing a service outage or critical issue, our emergency support team is available 24/7 for existing customers.
